WHO ARE WE?
We are a group of energetic young members of society who wish to preserve the world for generations to come.
Save the embryo, as a platform, enables over 20,000 volunteers across 10 cities in Canada to spread awareness and collaborate to
stand against global warming and deforestation by running campaigns to grow trees countrywide.
